The video project was used to show us how to make well thought out videos. We created a plan for the video and planned each scene. We than took time to shoot each video, and make edits to the script that we thought may improve it. We than took all of our audio and video files and edited them into the video linked above. The project showed us how to plan, create, and edit our own video.
