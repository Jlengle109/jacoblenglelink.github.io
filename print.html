Letort Bookmark

Pirate Bookmark

This bookmark represents where I started with InDesign. A good start but obviously nothing perfect. The project was more so used to show us the process of printing. We were tasked with creating grouping each bookmark with 4 others in our class and going through the steps of preparing the printer to print out multiple copies of each of our bookmarks. We were than taught how cut the down using the paper cutter. The bookmarks were later presented to Letort Elementary, and handed out to the kids.
